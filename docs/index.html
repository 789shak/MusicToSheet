How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

From audio file to printable sheet music in under a minute.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What audio formats do you support?
We support MP3, WAV, M4A, AAC, FLAC, and most common audio formats. You can also paste a direct link to an audio file hosted online.
How accurate is the transcription?
Our AI uses Spotify's Basic Pitch for polyphonic note detection combined with Meta's Demucs for stem separation. Accuracy depends on recording quality — clean recordings with a single instrument produce the best results.
Can I transcribe copyrighted music?
You are responsible for having the necessary rights to transcribe audio you upload. Our Terms of Service require a rights declaration before each conversion. All processing is ephemeral — audio is never stored on our servers.
What output formats are available?
Score, Part, Lead Sheet, Guitar Tabs, Fake Book, and Staff notation. You can export as PDF (all tiers), MIDI and MusicXML (Pro and Virtuosos tiers).
Do I need an account to use it?
No! Free users can transcribe up to 60 seconds of audio without creating an account. Sign up for longer transcriptions and access to your conversion history.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store account settings. There are no cancellation fees.
