Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: April 2026  ·  For users requesting account and data deletion

This page explains how to permanently delete your Music-To-Sheet account and all associated data. You can request deletion whether or not you still have access to the app.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account information (name, email, country, profile details)
  • All conversion history (uploaded track names, generated sheet music, favorites)
  • Rights declarations associated with your uploads
  • Purchase and subscription records (retained only where required by law for tax or accounting purposes, typically up to 7 years)
  • Usage and diagnostic data linked to your account

Audio files you uploaded are never retained — they are deleted immediately after processing completes (see our Privacy Policy for details).

How to Delete Your Account

There are two ways to request account deletion:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Fastest)

  • Open the Music-To-Sheet app
  • Go to Settings → Profile → Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion
  • Your account is deactivated immediately and permanently deleted within 30 days

Option 2: Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at support@musictosheet.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want your account and all associated data deleted

We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days, as required by applicable data protection law. You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete.

Data Retention After Deletion

After your deletion request is processed:

  • Account and profile data: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Conversion history and uploads: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies: Purged from all backups within an additional 30 days
  • Purchase records: Retained only where required by law (e.g., tax records for 7 years), in anonymised form only
Once deletion is complete you will not be able to recover your account, history, or any generated sheet music. If you wish to use Music-To-Sheet again after deletion, you will need to create a new account.

Contact

For deletion requests or questions about your data:

support@musictosheet.com

We aim to respond to all data requests within 30 days.
