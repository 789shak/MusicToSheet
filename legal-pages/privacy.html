Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026  ·  Effective upon account creation or first use

Music To Sheet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our mobile app and website, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1 Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and send important updates.
  • Authentication provider ID — a unique identifier from Google, Microsoft, or Apple when you sign in using those services. We do not receive or store your social account password.
  • Display name (optional) — if you choose to set one.

Audio Content You Upload

  • Audio files or links you submit for conversion are processed ephemerally.
  • Files are transmitted to our processing server solely to perform the conversion, and are permanently deleted immediately after conversion completes.
  • We do not store, archive, or retain copies of your audio for any purpose.
  • Your audio is never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI or machine learning model.

Usage Data

  • App interactions (features used, screens visited, conversion counts)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash and error reports

We do not collect precise location, biometric data, contact lists, or browsing history.

Purchase and Subscription Data

  • Current subscription plan tier
  • Transaction IDs and subscription status (via RevenueCat)
  • Purchase history within the app

Payment card details are handled exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play — we never see or store your payment credentials.

Conversion history (track name, instrument, output format, creation date) is stored in your account so you can access past results. This does not include the original audio file.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the audio-to-sheet-music conversion service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Verify and enforce your subscription plan and entitlements
  • Display your conversion history within the app
  • Respond to support requests and communicate service-related updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app performance and reliability
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3 Data Storage and Security

Storage

Your account data and conversion history are stored on Supabase, a managed database platform that provides encrypted storage at rest and encrypted transmission in transit (TLS 1.2+). Row-level security policies ensure your data is accessible only to your account.

Audio Processing

Audio files are processed on a strictly temporary basis and deleted immediately after conversion. We have no ability to retrieve your audio after processing completes. No audio is cached, archived, or backed up.

Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS
  • Database access is restricted by row-level security policies
  • Access controls limit which systems and personnel can access your data
  • No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

4 Legal Basis for Processing (EU/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract: Processing your account data, audio files, and conversion history is necessary to provide the App and fulfil our Terms of Service.
  • Legitimate interests: Processing usage and diagnostic data to detect fraud, prevent abuse, secure the App, and improve product quality. You may object to this processing at any time (see Section 6, Your Rights).
  • Consent: Where we rely on your consent (for example, optional profile information such as social media handles), you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law, including tax, accounting, or law-enforcement requests.

If you have questions about the legal basis for any specific processing activity, contact us at support@musictosheet.com.

6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Delete your account and all associated data directly in the app, or by emailing us. Completed within 30 days.

Portability

Receive your conversion history in a portable format on request.

Objection

Object to processing based on our legitimate interests.

Restriction

Request that we pause processing of your data in certain circumstances.

GDPR (EU / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, the above rights apply to you under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR. Our legal bases for processing are: contract performance (to provide the service you signed up for), legitimate interests (fraud prevention and app improvement), and compliance with legal obligations.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your local EU authority) if you are dissatisfied with how we handle your data.

CCPA (California Users)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale — we do not sell personal information; there is nothing to opt out of
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate for exercising any CCPA right

To submit a CCPA request, email support@musictosheet.com with subject "CCPA Request".

7 Children's Privacy

Music To Sheet is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe your child has created an account, please contact us at support@musictosheet.com and we will take immediate action.

Users aged 13–17 should have parental or guardian consent before using the app.
